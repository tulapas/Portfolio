Witch's Cave

   
                     
   
     

In Witch’s Cave, I acted as the main responsible for the game design and level design, structuring all the progression logic and challenge dynamics of the game.

     

I created the environments for each phase, carefully planning the layout and arrangement of elements to ensure a balanced difficulty curve. The game is structured around real-time order management, and my design proposal aimed precisely at that: positioning ingredients and scenario elements in a way that required quick thinking and space optimization.

     

I also developed the magical recipe system, thinking of intuitive and fun combinations, while also leaving room for strategic decisions about the positioning and use of resources.

     

Furthermore, I was responsible for all the creation of the pixel art assets used in the game and for their **integration within the engine**, ensuring visual and technical consistency between the phases.
