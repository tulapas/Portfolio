Right Round

   
                     
   
     

In Right Round, I acted as the main responsible for the game design and level design. From the beginning, my focus was to create an immersive, fluid, and engaging experience, developing systems and environments that evolved in harmony with the player.

     

I conceived and implemented central mechanics and progression systems, carefully introduced throughout the game to maintain a constant and satisfactory learning curve. Each new challenge or interaction was designed to continue the player's journey, without ruptures in immersion.

     

As the responsible for the complete level design, I divided the game map into 7 distinct areas, each with its own atmosphere and challenges, following the pace of the narrative and mechanics.

           

Furthermore, I developed a rhythm chart that maps the distribution of events, NPCs, introduction of mechanics, objectives, and narrative evolution. This feature was essential to ensure balance and cadence throughout the experience.

           

I also developed and programmed the quest system, which acts as a natural guide for the player, keeping them oriented and engaged with the story's progression. In addition, I implemented fundamental mechanics such as gliding, death, respawn, checkpoints, and item collection and storage.

     

Finally, I was responsible for all the integration of assets and systems within the engine, ensuring that all elements worked in an integrated and harmonious way.
