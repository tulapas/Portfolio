Space Gear

   
                     
   
     

Space Gear was a solo project developed as a practical study of a game with infinite progression. The goal was to create a simple yet functional experience, with a focus on difficulty scaling.

     

I was responsible for **the entire game production**, from design to programming. I worked on the **enemy strength tuning**, **scoring system**, and the **progression speed**, ensuring the challenge gradually increased as the player advanced.

     

This project allowed me to exercise fundamental skills in game loops, balancing, and feedback — essential aspects for any game development.

     

Link: https://tulapas.itch.io/space-gear
