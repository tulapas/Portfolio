About me

 

My name is Thalles Paiva, and more than a game developer, I am someone driven by the desire to create worlds where people can feel free, welcomed, and alive.

 

A graduate in Digital Games, I have been walking a path that unites technique, creativity, and purpose. I have experience as a game designer, level designer, and creative writer for artificial intelligence — and everything I do carries the same focus: to provoke real emotions, build memorable experiences, and, if possible, transform a bit of the world for those who play.

 

Today, I seek to expand this mission by diving into areas such as Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, not only to broaden my knowledge but to find new ways to touch people through technology.

 

I believe games are more than entertainment — they are bridges, they are refuges, they are hope. And that is why I keep creating.
