Project Alpha

   
                     
   
     

In Project Alpha, I was responsible for the creation of all levels and puzzles of the game, building an experience focused on reasoning and discovery in a lonely and devastated world.

     

I developed the structure of the phases, carefully distributing the mechanics with a rising difficulty curve. Each puzzle was designed to stimulate observation, trial-and-error, and logic.

     

I was also the creator of all the pixel art in the game, establishing a post-apocalyptic setting with cold tones and worn visual elements.

     

I finalized the process with the integration of all assets and systems in the engine, ensuring a cohesive and functional experience.
